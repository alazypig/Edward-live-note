`/i:/` 和 `/ɪ/`

eg:
- `ɪ` 可看做 `[ɪ] = [i] + [e]`
	- big `/bɪɡ/`
	- it `/ɪt/`
	- is `/ɪz/` (strong)
	- hit `/hɪt/`
	- fit `/fɪt/`
	- sit `/sɪt/`
	- shit `/ʃɪt/`
	- sick `/sɪk/`
	- it is so big
- `/i:/`
	- be `/biː/` (strong) `/bi/` (weak)
	- bee `/bi:/`
	- eat `/i:t/`
	- heat `/hiːt/`
	- feet `/fi:t/`
	- seat `/si:t/`
	- sheet `/ʃiːt/`
	- seek `/si:k/`
